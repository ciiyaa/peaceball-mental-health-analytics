# Caregiver Consent Form

## Peaceball Project - Wellbeing and Football Participation Study

Your daughter is invited to take part in a short wellbeing survey. The study focuses on understanding how daily life, stress, and access to football affect the wellbeing of girls living inside displacement camps in Kurdistan.

## What Participation Involves

- A supervised, 10-15 minute survey  
- No sensitive or personal-identifying questions  
- Participation is voluntary, and your daughter may stop at any time

## Confidentiality

All responses are anonymous. No names or identifying details will be recorded. Data will be stored securely and used only for research and reporting purposes.

## Risks and Benefits

There are no direct risks. Some questions may feel personal, but none touch on traumatic events. The study may help improve future programs that support girls' wellbeing and access to safe activities.

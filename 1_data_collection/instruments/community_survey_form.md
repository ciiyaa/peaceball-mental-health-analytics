# Community Pride & Football Identity Survey

Peaceball Project – Short Community Questionnaire  
(Used during the Zakho FIFA Best Fan Award nomination period)

This short form documents the community survey used to understand how football influences pride, mood, and collective identity among residents of Zakho and nearby areas. It was designed as a light-touch tool requiring no written consent, since no personal identifiers or sensitive items were collected.

---

## **1. Purpose of the Tool**

The survey measures:

- Awareness of the Zakho FIFA nomination  
- Emotional impact of football on daily mood  
- Community pride and sense of belonging  
- Patterns across gender and age groups

---

## **2. Question Breakdown**

### **Section A - Awareness & Engagement**

1. **Did you know about Zakho SC's FIFA fan nomination?**  
   - Yes  
   - No  
   - I heard about it but didn't follow it  

2. **Did you vote in the nomination?**  
   - Yes  
   - No  
   - I couldn't / didn't have access  

---

### **Section B - Emotional Response to Football**

Scale:
5 = Very strong positive feeling  
4 = Strong  
3 = Moderate  
2 = Weak  
1 = Not at all

1. Supporting Zakho SC makes me feel hopeful.  
2. Football helps me cope with stress.  
3. Watching matches improves my mood.  

---

### **Section C - Identity & Belonging**

Scale 1–5 as above.

1. I feel proud to be from Zakho.  
2. Football brings the community together.  
3. I feel connected to other fans.

## **3. Enumerator Notes**

- The form is anonymous.  
- No signatures, names, or addresses collected.  
- Participation is verbal and fully voluntary.  
- Designed as a rapid-pulse community perception survey.
